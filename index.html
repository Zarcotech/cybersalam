Our Team

Our team is a group of students from different schools in the area. We are a part of the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) program and compete in the FTC competitions. We are a rookies and this is our first year competing, excited to learn and grow as a team.












Our Robot

Our robot is named "Salamander". It is a robot that can pick up and stack blocks. It can also move around the field and climb onto the platform. We have worked hard to design and build our robot and we are excited to see how it performs in the competitions.
